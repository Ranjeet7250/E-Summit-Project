TRADING WORKSHOP/ SIMULATION

Stock trading simulations offer a risk-free way to learn trading. With real-time data, diverse options, and performance tracking, they help users understand the market and improve financial literacy. These simulations enhance decision-making skills and confidence, making them essential for aspiring traders.1-day guided workshop with supply of study material that familiarizes them with the basic terms and advanced techniques of Stock Investing by the guest trainer. The Stock Market Simulation Event will take place for 5 days

RULES AND REGULATIONS

  • The Simulation game will be of 5 days.
  • Each participant will be given a base of 5 Lacs INR (virtual money cannot be withdrawn) at the start of the event that they will be trading.
  • Stocks of around 100 companies will be added while creating the game. The companies may be taken from NSE, BSE, or other stock exchanges of cities of India.

TARGET AUDIENCE

  • Students from all across India
  • Participating must be an Postgraduate student or below.

BENEFITS FOR PARTICIPANTS

  • CASH PRIZE (10k, 8k,5k)
  • Certificates to all the Participants
