WEB DESIGNING COMPETITION

An ad mad show is a creative competition where teams of participants design and present humorous advertisements for various products, services, or social themes given on spot to the teams and with limited time to prepare.

RULES AND REGULATIONS

  • Team of 2-5 Participants
  • 1 Girl in the team in compulsion
  • 30 Minutes will be given to Prepare
  • Judges will evaluate the advertisement based on its creativity, including the use of innovative ideas and execution.

TARGET AUDIENCE

  • College Going Student
  • Freelance developers

BENEFITS FOR PARTICIPANTS

  • CASH PRIZE (10K, 8K, 5K)
  • Certificates to all the Participants
  • Partner Benefits
