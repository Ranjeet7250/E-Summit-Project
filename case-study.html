CASE STUDY COMPETITION

A case study competition, in partnership with a startup or established company, tasks participants with solving real-world business challenges. Participants develop and present their solutions to a panel of experts, fostering critical thinking and nurturing an entrepreneurial mindset. This competition bridges the gap between theory and practice, encouraging innovative problem-solving and practical application of business concepts.

RULES AND REGULATIONS

  • Teams of 2-4 members can participate
  • Submissions should include a written report and a presentation.
  • 10 Minutes will be given to pitch
  • Solutions will be judged based on creativity, feasibility, analysis, and presentation quality.
  • Feedback will be provided to all teams.

TARGET AUDIENCE

  • UG/ PG Students

BENEFITS FOR PARTICIPANTS

  • CASH PRIZE (10k, 8k,5k)
  • Certificates to each team.
  • Connect with industry professionals and like-minded peers.
